Name Card Website

Your online business card

This used a site template from HTML5UP, and some CSS and vanilla Javascript.

A simply structured but beautiful looking business card website, this is perfect for directing people to the social tags you want people to have access to in order to get in touch with you or see projects you have worked on.

This project was one of my more straight forward ones, but I still like the finished product, and I think functionally this can be a great first impression when networking, showing people your digital business card!
